The Smallest 3D Maker

The 10k way to get your beautiful 3d wireframe!

Edit Mode Tips

  • Click into red square to create a new control point;
  • Create at least two control points;
  • You can drag a control point to modify the Bezier Curve;
  • Maintain the mouse pointer over a control point and press DEL to delete; and
  • Press ENTER when finish.

View Mode Tips

  • Use the arrow keys to rotate your wireframe;
  • Press ESC to return to the edit mode.
